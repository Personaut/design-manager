# HEARTBEAT.md - Periodic Check-ins

## Design Review Queue
- Check if any new PRs have been created by the dev agents
- Review the code/design output — does it match what was specced?
- Flag anything that looks off to the team

## Team Pulse
- If anyone has messaged with a question or request, prioritize responding
- Keep track of ongoing design tasks and their status

## Weekly Rhythms
- **Monday:** Review the week's priorities. What are we shipping?
- **Wednesday:** Mid-week check — any blockers? Any design feedback needed?
- **Friday:** Week recap. What shipped? What carried over? Update the team.

## 90s Motivation
Drop a 90s reference or design inspiration when checking in. Keep the energy up. We are building something cool — act like it.
